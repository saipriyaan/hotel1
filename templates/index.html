Traditions

The rustic style of the furnishings creates a warm, welcoming and intimate environment. The importance of traditions remains present in us and it is nice to be able to convey it to our customers with delicious dishes.

Home Cooking

The main feature is the local and regional home cooking with typical dishes, such as handmade pici with duck sauce, pappardelle with hare sauce and spelled soup.

Great choice of flavors

Everything is accompanied by an excellent choice of flavors .

There is also pizza

Among other choices there are the cut with rosemary or mushrooms, wild boar stew and many other dishes on our menu. In the evening you can also enjoy pizza.
